Our Story

Who We Are

Your trusted freight partner across Canada and the USA. Family-owned, BC-based, and built on standards that show up in every load we haul.

Founded 2019 · Abbotsford, BC

A Carrier Known
for Its Standards.

Keylink Transport Inc. has 15 years of accumulated experience with a straightforward conviction: the freight industry didn't need another big carrier, it needed a reliable one.

Based in Abbotsford, British Columbia, we specialize in dry van full-truckload freight across Canada and the United States. As a family-owned and operated carrier, every decision we make is shaped by one question: would we be comfortable if our client could see exactly what we're doing?

We're USDOT certified, ICBC insured, and 5-star rated by the industry brokers and businesses who move freight with us. We operate from two offices, Abbotsford, BC and Calgary, AB, covering lanes across Western Canada and the US West Coast.

Core Values

The Five Things We Never Compromise On.

Reliability
We deliver when we say we will. No excuses, no rescheduling, no surprises. Your timeline is our commitment.
Safety
Modern equipment, trained drivers, and strict safety protocols on every run. We don't cut corners on the road. Ever.
Communication
Transparency in all dealings. Real-time updates, full documentation, and one direct contact for every shipment.
Efficiency
Simplified freight management that saves you time and up to 90% vs carrier direct pricing. No complexity, no runaround.
