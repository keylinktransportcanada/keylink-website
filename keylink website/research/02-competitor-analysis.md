## Patterns of the Top 10%

These are the 5 non-negotiable patterns found on every elite freight carrier site:

### Pattern 1: STAT STRIP ABOVE THE FOLD
Every top performer puts quantified proof in the hero. Not claims — numbers.
- First Frontier: "200K+ Successful Shipments | 25+ Years | 25% Cost Reduction"
- This is the single biggest gap in KeyLink's current site — their 1,500 truckloads/month stat is buried in a bullet list.

### Pattern 2: CERTIFICATION/COMPLIANCE BADGES VISIBLE IMMEDIATELY
Elite carriers make compliance visible. USDOT, MC#, ICBC, CT-PAT, Haz-Mat certifications are shown as visual badges — not buried in text. ET Transport shows 8 badges in the first scroll. KeyLink mentions these in a bullet list only.

### Pattern 3: MULTIPLE EXPLICIT CTAs — QUOTE + PHONE
Top sites use dual CTAs: "Get a Quote" (form) + a phone number click-to-call. This captures both research-mode buyers and ready-to-book buyers. KeyLink only has a "Get a Quote" button.

### Pattern 4: REAL PHOTOGRAPHY + VIDEO
Stock photos are the enemy of trust in freight. The best sites use real fleet photography and company culture video. First Frontier uses real truck-on-highway shots. ET Transport has a YouTube company film. KeyLink's hero uses an AI-generated image and a video that fails to load.

### Pattern 5: DEDICATED SERVICE PAGES WITH SEO COPY
Top performers rank on page 1 for terms like "FTL carrier BC," "cross-border dry van Canada-USA," etc. because they have dedicated service pages with content. KeyLink has no service pages — every service is compressed into the homepage.

## KeyLink's Competitive Opportunity

KeyLink has genuinely strong differentiators that competitors lack:
1. **"Save up to 90% off carrier direct pricing"** — no competitor makes this claim
2. **5-Star rated by industry brokers** — social proof that matters to B2B buyers
3. **Family-owned + personal service** — authentic differentiator vs. large carriers
4. **BC-based with US coverage to Sacramento** — strong geographic niche
5. **$750,000 BIPD insured** — higher than typical; should be displayed as a badge

The new site's job: make all 5 of these impossible to miss.
