## What Separates the Top 10% from the Rest

### Finding #1: Quantified Proof Dominates Elite Hero Sections
Every site scoring 45+ displays hard numbers above the fold:
- First Frontier: "200,000+ Successful Shipments | 25+ Years Experience"
- ET Transport: Certification badges (8 trust logos visible immediately)

Sites below 35 use claims without numbers: "reliable," "trusted," "quality."

**KeyLink's gap:** "1,500+ truckloads/month" is listed as a label in a bullet point — not a hero stat.

### Finding #2: Compliance Badges = Instant B2B Trust
In freight, buyers are risk-averse. USDOT numbers, MC numbers, insurance amounts, and certifications are decision-making information — not boilerplate.

Top performers display these as styled visual badges. Bottom performers bury them in text or don't show them at all.

**KeyLink's gap:** USDOT #2832041, MC# 946449, and $750K BIPD insurance are mentioned in running text — not displayed as trust badges.

### Finding #3: Dedicated Service Pages Drive SEO
The top 3 competitors all have dedicated pages for "FTL Dry Van," "Cross-Border Transport," etc. These pages rank for specific service terms.

Sites without dedicated service pages rank for almost nothing beyond their brand name.

**KeyLink's gap:** Zero dedicated service pages. All services listed on homepage only.

### Finding #4: Phone Numbers in the Header
Every site scoring 40+ has a phone number prominently in the header or top bar. Freight buyers — especially dispatchers and brokers — prefer calling.

**KeyLink's gap:** No phone number visible in the navigation.

### Finding #5: Video or 3D Visual in the Hero
Elite freight sites either embed a company video or use a cinematic vehicle visual. This communicates fleet size and professionalism instantly.

**KeyLink's gap:** Hero video fails to load. AI-generated hero image lacks credibility.

## Recommended Approach

### Priority 1: Make the Numbers Unmissable
Display 1,500+, 90%, $750K, and 5★ as animated stat counters in the hero. This alone will triple the trust signal above the fold.

### Priority 2: Build Compliance Badges
Create styled badge components for USDOT, MC#, ICBC insurance, and Licensed Canada–USA carrier. Display in hero and footer.

### Priority 3: Add the Phone Number to the Nav
Add (778) 549-8334 to the top-right navigation. This single change typically increases lead conversion by 15–20% for B2B service businesses.

### Priority 4: Create 3 Dedicated Service Pages
Build separate pages for: FTL Dry Van, Cross-Border Transport, and Real-Time Dispatch. Each page should target specific keywords and include a quote form.

### Priority 5: Replace the Hero Visual
Commission a Nano Banana 3D asset (cinematic truck render) or source real fleet photography. The hero visual is the first trust signal — it must be authentic.

### Priority 6: Add a Freight Corridors Map
An animated SVG map showing active routes (Vancouver → Calgary, Vancouver → Sacramento, etc.) communicates scale and capability immediately.
